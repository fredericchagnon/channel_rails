Set your availability

Some people prefer receiving text messages; others want to be Skyped. We each have our preferences, and they change based on what we're doing.

Channel lets you define as many statuses as you want, and lets you set them with one click. Each status translates to an ordered list of "channels" on which you want to be reached.

Your contacts will always know how you want to be reached.

 

Differentiate your access

Maybe you prefer that your friends text you while you're at work, but you want your work colleagues to call you on your work Blackberry.

Channel allows you to group your contacts and customize availability for the different groups. For example, set your status to "At Work", and your friends might be prompted to text you while your "Professional Contacts" will know to call your Blackberry.

Channel gives you two groups out of the box. We plan on letting you add more groups in the future.

 

Manage your information

We move, we change jobs... Our addresses, telephone numbers, email addresses change in time.

Whether you're adding a new phone number or editing an email address, your contacts will always have the latest. No need for mass emails or facebook wall posts and hoping that people update their phones!

 

Avoid useless voicemails

Let's face it, most voicemails we get are a variation on "Hey, it's me. Call me back when you get a chance."

With Channel, you know when someone isn't available to talk. Simply click a button and "leave a message" for them to call you back...

When you receive a "Contact Back" request, just click on it and you'll be presented with how that person wants to be contacted.
